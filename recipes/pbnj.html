Peanut Butter and Jelly

Everyone loves this favorite classic, a PB&J!

Whether you're 5 or 50, this sandwich continues to bring joy to people nationwide

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Place 2 slices of bread on a plate(these can be toasted if you prefer)
  2. Take a butter knife, and spread some peanut butter evenly over one slice
  3. After cleaning that butter knife, take some jelly and do a similar maneuver to the other slice
  4. Place the two slices of bread together, so the peanut butter and jelly are combined in the middle, and enjoy!
