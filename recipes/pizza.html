Pizza Time

Here is how to make the best mostly homemade pizza!

It's a fairly straightforward process, that the whole family can take part in, and will be filling bellies in the matter of about 20-30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Any and all preferred toppings(Peppers, sausages, pepperoni, mushrooms), I prefer bacon and pineapple on mine!
  • Preferred seasonings

Steps

  1. Flatten your pizza dough or prepare your base
  2. Apply some tomato sauce in the middle, and take a spoon, slowly making swirls and and expanding it out
  3. Apply a layer of shredded Mozzarella
  4. Add any additional toppings. Be as generous or light as you prefer
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes, depending on preferred doneness
  6. Remove from oven, let sit for about 5 minutes, slice and enjoy!
