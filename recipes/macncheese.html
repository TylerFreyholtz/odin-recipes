Macaroni and Cheese

Everyone is sure to love this Macaroni and Cheese dish!

Enjoy this delicious Macaroni and Cheese that is sure to impress everyone!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni noodles(whichever kind you prefer)
  • Multiple different types of cheese, I usually aim for 3
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt, Pepper, and any other preferred seasonings

Steps

  1. Heat a pot of water on the stovetop
  2. Once boiling, add your desired amount of noodles, and cook 7-8 minutes until done
  3. Strain water from noodles
  4. While pan is empty, add a dash of milk, butter, and as much cheese as your heart desires, mixing until melted
  5. Add noodles back in, mixing until combined
  6. Top with seasonings and enjoy!
